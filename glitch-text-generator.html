Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is glitch text and how is it created?
Glitch text, also known as Zalgo text or corrupted text, is text that appears visually distorted or corrupted through the strategic use of Unicode combining characters. These special characters stack above, below, and through normal letters to create the appearance of digital corruption or glitching. Our generator automates the complex process of selecting and positioning these characters to create professional glitch effects instantly.
Is glitch text safe to use on social media platforms?
Yes, glitch text is completely safe to use. It's created using standard Unicode characters that are supported across all major platforms. However, individual platforms may have policies about excessive formatting or spam, so use glitch text appropriately and avoid overuse that might be flagged as disruptive behavior. The text itself poses no security risk.
Will glitch text work on my smartphone?
Absolutely. Glitch text works perfectly on iOS, Android, and all mobile devices that support Unicode text (which is virtually all modern smartphones).
